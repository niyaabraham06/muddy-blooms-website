Common Questions About Our Services

What services does Muddy Blooms offer?

Muddy Blooms specializes in comprehensive landscaping design and installation, maintenance services, and providing a wide variety of indoor plants for homes and businesses. We also offer expert advice on plant care and sustainable green solutions.

Where are your services available?

We primarily serve customers across Kerala, with a strong presence in major cities and surrounding areas. Please visit our Locations page for a detailed list of areas we cover.

Do you provide custom landscape designs?

Yes, absolutely! We pride ourselves on creating bespoke landscape designs tailored to your specific preferences, site conditions, and budget. Our team works closely with you from concept to completion.

How often do I need to water my indoor plants?

Watering frequency depends on the plant species, pot size, light conditions, and humidity. Generally, it's best to check the soil moisture. Most plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. We provide specific care instructions with each plant purchase.

What is your process for a new landscaping project?

Our process typically involves an initial consultation, site assessment, conceptual design, detailed proposal, material selection, installation, and post-installation care guidance. We ensure a smooth and transparent journey for every client.
