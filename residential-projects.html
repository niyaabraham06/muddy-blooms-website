The Thekkumkattil Residence - Serene Oasis

Client: Mr. Subin Mathew| Location: [Kottayam,Kerala] | Time Period: [February 2024 - August 2024]

This project was a true collaboration with Mr. and Mrs. Subin Thekkumkattil, who were building their new home. We had the privilege of handling their complete landscaping work, focusing on creating a harmonious blend of indoor and outdoor greenery. We meticulously placed plants inside their home to enhance living spaces and designed a tranquil garden oasis outside, ensuring a seamless connection with nature.

  • Comprehensive indoor plantscaping design and installation
  • Custom garden design and outdoor plant placement
  • Low-maintenance native plant selection for sustainability
