Discover the passion and purpose behind Muddy Blooms.

Our Journey from a Seed of an Idea

Muddy Blooms began in 2018 with a simple, yet profound love for nature and a vision to bring green spaces to life. What started as a small local nursery and a passion for gardening has blossomed into a comprehensive landscaping and plant solutions provider.

We saw the potential to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary natural retreats, whether it's a serene home garden in Kottayam, a vibrant landscape for a luxury resort, or enhancing commercial properties with bespoke plant designs. Our journey has been defined by continuous learning, dedication to sustainable practices, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' visions.
